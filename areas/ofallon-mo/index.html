What we build in O'Fallon

Most O'Fallon homes were built between the late 1990s and the mid-2010s — meaning a lot of original wood decks are starting to fail, a lot of original bathrooms are due for a refresh, and a lot of kitchens were designed with a wall in the middle that doesn't need to be there anymore. That's the work we do.

Decks & Outdoor

Composite deck builds & rescues

Full deck replacements with composite boards, aluminum rails, picture-frame finishes, and lattice skirting. When the joists or ledger are rotted, we rebuild from the foundation up — including the door, flashing, and trim around the opening.

Bathrooms

Full bathroom remodels

Gut renovations with proper roof venting (no more dumping moisture into the attic), waterproofing, re-routed plumbing with shut-off valves, custom tile niches, and recessed medicine cabinets. Two to four weeks start to finish.

Kitchens

Open-up wall removals & counters

Non-load-bearing walls down, electrical re-routed, custom butcher block in hevea or maple with weather-oak or natural finish, extended runs that wrap removed walls for more leg room and a fresh look.

Carpentry & Built-ins

Stairs, mantels, structural repairs

Custom oak staircases with metal balusters and thicker newel posts. Hand-built stone mantels with hidden coax, power, and Ethernet runs for clean AV installs. Rotted subfloor replacement and bay window rebuilds.
