Serving St. Charles, Missouri

Renovation, remodeling & carpentry for St. Charles homeowners.

We're based in O'Fallon, MO, fifteen minutes west of St. Charles, and we work in St. Charles regularly. Older homes near Main Street and Frenchtown often need structural work the original contractor skipped — rotted subfloors, undersized headers, exhaust vents that just dump moisture into the attic. Newer subdivisions out toward New Town and Cottleville hit the same wall: original wood decks failing, original bathrooms aging out, original kitchens designed around walls that don't earn their keep anymore.

Either way, you get the same crew, the same fixed quote, and the same job site that gets cleaned up at the end of every day.
