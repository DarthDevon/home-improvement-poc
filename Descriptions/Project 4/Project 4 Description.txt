Before, after and during photos of the latest bathroom.   - We gutted the bathroom, removed old soffits that took up a lot of space
- Added insulation
- Re framed the wall to match the current wall
- Cut open roof for vent since the old exhaust vent was just dumping moisture into the attic
- Re routed the wiring from the soffits,
- Cut out the old plumbing installed new plumbing with shut off valves directly under
- Made custom niches for the recessed medicine cabinet and shower,
- All new drywall, finishing and painting. 
- Waterproofed the shower and niche and tiled
- Cement boarding for floor with new tile
It’s been a heck of a process but it came together nicely
