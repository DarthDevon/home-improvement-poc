We had a request to tear out the carpet and trim from a seemingly unsafe stair case. The previous owners had a 1” x8” tripping hazard at the top of the stairs along with very wobbly stair rail. We tore it all out and started from scratch with new oak treads and risers. New thicker stair newel and rails and metal balusters to match. There were special order parts we had to make work but We have enjoyed the challenge of lining everything up and making it smooth. 

There’s a lot of pictures but it’s quite a process. We even had to take out the entire entryway flooring and trim just to be able to make the floor re line up where the old tripping hazard was since there was no floor under.
