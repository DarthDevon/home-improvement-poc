This is a counter top replacement project from last week. We took down a non load bearing wall, moved the electrical, mending together and cut a custom to the house butcher block made from hevea and finished it in a weather oak finish. 
We added extra width and length so it would go over the wall that was taken down, give a little extra leg room and extended it to wrap around the wall. 
It opened up the whole living space and gave a fresh new look.
