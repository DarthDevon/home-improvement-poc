HOW TO SEND ME YOUR FACEBOOK PAGE CONTENT FOR THE NEW WEBSITE
================================================================

Hey Mike — to build the new site, I need the photos, videos, and text
descriptions that are already on your Facebook page. Here are two ways

If you only have time for one of them, do OPTION 1 — it's the most

--------------------------------------------------------------------
OPTION 1 — DOWNLOAD A FULL EXPORT FROM FACEBOOK (RECOMMENDED)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Best done on a desktop or laptop computer, NOT the phone app.
This gets ALL your photos, posts, captions, and videos in one zip file.

  STEP 1. Open facebook.com in a web browser and log in.

  STEP 2. Switch to your Howe Renovations Page (not your personal
          profile). Click your profile picture in the top-right
          corner, then click "Switch" or "See all profiles," then
          pick the Howe Renovations Page.

  STEP 3. Once you're on the Page, click your Page profile picture
          (top right) and choose "Settings & privacy" → "Settings."

  STEP 4. In the left-hand menu, look for a section called
          "Your information" or "Your Facebook information."
          Click "Download your information" (it may also be labeled
          "Download a copy of your information" or "Download profile
          information").

  STEP 5. Click "Request a download" or "Create file."
          On the options screen:
            - Date range:    "All time"
            - Format:        HTML (easier to read) — or JSON if it
                             asks; either works
            - Media quality: HIGH
            - Select what to include: check at least these boxes:
                * Posts
                * Photos and videos
                * Comments
                * About you / Page info
                (If you're not sure, just check "Select all.")

  STEP 6. Submit the request. Facebook will email you (and put a
          notification in Facebook) when the file is ready —
          usually within an hour, sometimes a day if the Page is big.

  STEP 7. When you get the "Your download is ready" notification,
          go back to the same "Download your information" screen,
          click "Available files," and download the ZIP file to
          your computer.

  STEP 8. Send me the ZIP using one of these (it will be too big
          for email):
            - Google Drive   (right-click → Share → send link)
            - Dropbox        (upload, then share link)
            - WeTransfer.com (free, no account, up to 2GB)
          Send the link to: devonstreckfuss@gmail.com


--------------------------------------------------------------------
OPTION 2 — QUICK PHONE METHOD (IF OPTION 1 IS TOO MUCH)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
If the export is giving you trouble, this works too — it just takes
more taps.

  STEP 1. Open the Facebook app on your phone.

  STEP 2. Go to the Howe Renovations Page.

  STEP 3. Tap "Photos" (or "Media" depending on the app version).

  STEP 4. For each album:
            - Open the album
            - Tap a photo
            - Tap the three dots (⋯) in the top right
            - Tap "Save to phone" or "Save photo"
            - Swipe to the next photo and repeat
          (If you have a LOT of photos, just send the best 30–50
          that show your range of work — full kitchen remodels,
          bathrooms, decks, etc. We don't need every single one.)

  STEP 5. Take a screenshot of any post that has a good description
          of a project — the words you wrote matter as much as the
          photos.

  STEP 6. Send everything to me via:
            - Google Photos shared album   (easiest from phone)
            - Google Drive
            - Or text/iMessage if it's under ~20 photos
          To: devonstreckfuss@gmail.com  or  text my phone

--------------------------------------------------------------------

You can just text me these or type them in an email — whatever's
easier. No essays needed, bullet points are fine.

  1. Services you offer (e.g. kitchen remodels, bathroom remodels,

  2. Service area — which towns / counties / how far you'll travel.

  4. What makes your work different from the next guy — in your

     and whether you want a "Request a Demo / Estimate" form on
     the site (I'm planning to build one, but I want to make sure
     the leads go to the right place).

  6. Any reviews or testimonials from happy customers you'd like
     featured. Screenshots from Google / Facebook reviews are fine.

  7. Your logo file if you have one. If you don't, no worries —
     I can design something simple.
